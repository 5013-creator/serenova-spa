About Serenova Spa & Wellness

Serenova Spa & Wellness has been proudly serving our community since 2007. We are a locally owned and operated day spa and we take great pride in providing a warm, welcoming, and professional environment for all of our guests.

We offer a full menu of services including Swedish massage, deep tissue massage, hot stone massage, prenatal massage, signature facials, anti-aging treatments, body wraps, waxing, manicures, pedicures, and so much more. We also carry a full line of retail spa products that make perfect gifts!!

Our staff are all licensed and certified professionals who truly love what they do. We believe that self care is not a luxury it is a necessity, and we are here to help you take care of yourself!! Whether you are treating yourself or buying a gift for someone special, Serenova is the place to be.

We accept walk-ins based on availability but appointments are always recommended especially on weekends. You deserve it!!
